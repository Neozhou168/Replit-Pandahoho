# See Beijing from Above

Beijing, China•Attractions•Best in All seasons

Favorite

Look Beijing From the Top takes you to the city’s most breathtaking viewpoints — from Jingshan Park’s hilltop, where the golden roofs of the Forbidden City stretch beneath your feet, to the sky-high elegance of China World Summit Wing, the modern heights of Olympic Tower, and the classic charm of the Central TV Tower. Discover how ancient majesty and futuristic skylines meet in one panoramic journey — the perfect route to feel Beijing’s true scale and spirit.
